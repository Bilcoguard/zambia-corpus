    About ZambiaLII

    ZambiaLII is a pioneer in the free access to law movement and serves as a reliable and timely source of judicial decisions, legislation and statutory instruments. Since 2012, ZambiaLII has been hosted by the Southern African Institute for Policy and Research (SAIPAR)opens in new tab, an independent, educational and development-oriented research centre. AfricanLII provides technical support to ZambiaLII.
